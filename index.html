hi there!

My name is Sarah Idahosa, and I am actively engaged in the web3 community building space, serving as a Token Economist, Web3 Educator, and an advocate for Decentralized Finance. One of my primary areas of expertise lies in educating people about blockchain technology, guiding them in their transition from web2 to web3.

As a seasoned public speaker, I have had the privilege of sharing my knowledge at esteemed events such as the Nigerian Fintech Week and the Africa Money and DeFi Summit in Ghana.

The pride of my accomplishments is as the founder of WomenInDefi, a thriving community that empowers and educates females about blockchain technology, while also providing insights on leveraging their skills within the web3 Ecosystem.

In my previous role, I worked with Mara, a startup still in its infancy without a revenue model. Through my dedication and the efforts of my team, we successfully cultivated a community of over 50k members on Telegram and garnered an impressive wait-list of 3 million signups through innovative ideas and contributions.

Currently, I am engaged with Cassava, where I am proactively onboarding communities onto the platform. Once again, starting from scratch, we have achieved remarkable results, attracting over 60 brands and communities in less than a month.
